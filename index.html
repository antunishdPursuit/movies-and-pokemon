Pokemon Battle

Build your team. Win the battle.

Choose a player, pick one Pokemon from the team for each round, and be the first to six wins.

How to play

  1. Choose Gardevoir, Lopunny, or Primarina as your player.
  2. Choose one Pokemon from your team.
  3. Hover over or select Fight to resolve the round.
  4. Win six rounds to win a battle. Win two battles to win the match.
